Records Cleanup Automation

Product Owner perspective on eliminating stale inventory noise

Product Ownership Process Automation Data Quality Stakeholder Management Power Automate

Context

Inventory reports were cluttered with records that were inactive or untouched for over a year. Stakeholders struggled to analyze data or make decisions because obsolete items distorted metrics and priorities.

Problem

Stale records remained visible to business and compliance teams, creating noise, inflating counts, and slowing decisions. Manual cleanup was ad hoc, inconsistent, and rarely prioritized.

Goal

Automate identification of stale records, engage record owners for confirmation, and decommission obsolete items so the inventory reflects only actionable, current records.

Stakeholders

  • Record owners
  • Business unit leads
  • Compliance / governance partners
  • Data & reporting teams
  • IT / platform admins

Product Owner Journey: Discovery → Decision → Implementation → Impact

1 Discovery

The Problem

  • 12+ months of inactive records cluttering views
  • Manual cleanup inconsistent & deprioritized
  • Noise slows stakeholder decisions
  • Compliance audit challenges
  • Reports inflated with historical data

The Goal

Automate stale record detection, engage owners, decommission obsolete items for clean, actionable inventory.

2 Decisions

Strategic Choices

  • Threshold: 12 months vs. 6 months
  • Engagement: Explicit approval vs. auto-delete
  • Notification: 2 reminders + timeout
  • Scope: MVP detection first, Phase 3 automation
  • Audit: Mandatory logging for compliance

3 Trade-offs

Gained vs. Lost

Approval Loop

✓ Trust & accountability | ✗ 30-day delay

12-Month Threshold

✓ Balanced approach | ✗ Slower cleanup

Audit Trail

✓ Full compliance | ✗ Storage overhead

Phased Rollout

✓ Stakeholder buy-in | ✗ Delayed ROI

4 Implementation

Solution Components

  • Detect: Flag 12+ month inactive records
  • Notify: Targeted owner confirmations
  • Decommission: Auto-remove after approval
  • Audit: Log all decisions & actions
  • Report: Feed clean data to dashboards

Phased Delivery

P1: Detection | P2: MVP + notifications | P3: Auto-decommission | P4: Reporting

5 Success & Risks

Metrics to Track

  • 85%+ stale records removed
  • 70%+ owner approval rate
  • 90%+ data freshness
  • 40% faster decisions
  • 30% ticket reduction

Key Risks & Mitigations

  • Non-compliance: Reminders + timeout policy
  • Data loss: Soft-delete + audit trail
  • Low adoption: Phased rollout + comms
  • Performance: Batch processing + indexing

6 Outcomes

Real Impact

  • Noise Reduction: Large backlog of stale records removed; improved data quality & signal-to-noise
  • Faster Decisions: Stakeholders act on cleaner data without wading through obsolete items
  • Accountability: Owners became engaged stewards; trust in inventory increased
