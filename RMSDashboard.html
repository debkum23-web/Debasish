SharePoint Governance Insight Dashboard for Business Units

Acting Product Owner / Principal Software Engineer

Power BI SharePoint Online Data Modeling Governance Stakeholder Discovery

Context

Business units needed a reliable, at-a-glance view of SharePoint usage to support governance, storage planning, and operational decisions.

Problem

Key details (site collection URL, record manager/business unit, library name, library size, item counts) lived in disparate sources. Manual compilations were slow and error-prone, limiting proactive management.

Goal

Give stakeholders a single, trusted dashboard that surfaces the most important inventory and usage signals quickly.

Users & Stakeholders

  • Business unit leads
  • Record managers
  • SharePoint owners
  • Governance/compliance partners

Solution

Designed and delivered a Power BI dashboard that aggregates and visualizes:

Site Collection URLs

Centralized view of all SharePoint site collections across the organization.

Record Manager & Business Unit

Clear ownership mapping for accountability and governance tracking.

Library Name

Complete inventory of document libraries for comprehensive oversight.

Library Size

Storage utilization metrics to support capacity planning and optimization.

Item Counts per Library

Volume tracking for content management and compliance monitoring.

Outcomes

30% Reduction

Cut manual reporting effort by 30%, freeing teams for strategic work.

Near Real-Time Monitoring

Enabled business units to monitor libraries in near real-time for proactive management.

50% Risk Reduction

Drove 50% reduction in capacity risks and non-compliant libraries through early visibility.

Tech & Practices

Power BI SharePoint Online Data Modeling Stakeholder Discovery Backlog Creation Iterative Delivery Governance Alignment

Approach

  • Discovery: Engaged business units to identify critical metrics and pain points
  • Data Integration: Consolidated disparate data sources into unified Power BI model
  • Iterative Delivery: Released dashboard incrementally with stakeholder feedback loops
  • Governance Alignment: Ensured compliance with organizational data policies

Artifacts & Evidence

Key Takeaways

This project demonstrated the value of consolidating disparate governance data into a single source of truth. By enabling near real-time visibility into SharePoint usage patterns, business units could shift from reactive firefighting to proactive capacity planning and compliance management. The iterative delivery approach ensured stakeholder buy-in and continuous refinement based on real-world usage patterns.
